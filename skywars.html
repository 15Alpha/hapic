Our take on the Skywars gamemode - featuring the Angel of Death. Each player/team spawns on its own island and the aim is to be the last player or team alive. Kill players using a weapon or knock them off.

With each kill you will be rewarded with a soul. You can use souls to unlock many kits and perks to improve your game experience.
